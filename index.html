Moonrise Kingdom (2012)

Another masterpiece from Wes Anderson! This movie will keep you glued to your seat for one and a half hour and will leave with a warm glow in your heart.

There is adventure, and comedy, and some drama, and growing up as an orphan in a small New England community, and even a kitten! The film received glowing reviews on Metacritic and on Rotten Tomatoes, as well as a score of 7.8 (out of 10) on IMDB.

The cast includes Edward Norton (as Scout Master Ward), Bruce Willis (as Captain Sharp -- the only sherif on the whole island), Bill Murray and Frances McDormand (as Mr. and Mrs Bishop -- a lawyer couple and parents of the main heroine), and others.
